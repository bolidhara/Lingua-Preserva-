Our Mission

At Lingua Preserva, we are deeply committed to the preservation and promotion of the world’s endangered and rare languages. Our mission is built on the belief that every language is a unique manifestation of culture, history, and identity, and an invaluable component of the world's linguistic diversity.

In today’s globalized world, language loss has reached alarming levels, with nearly 40% of the world’s languages at risk of vanishing within this century. This is a global crisis that threatens the cultural heritage embedded in these languages. At Lingua Preserva, we are determined to address this challenge independently by providing resources and platforms to document, study, and revitalize endangered languages.

Our Goals and Objectives

Our central goal is to create a comprehensive, accessible, and sustainable platform that serves as a repository for knowledge about endangered languages. We focus on the following key objectives:

  1. Documentation and Preservation: We work independently to collaborate with linguists, community leaders, and cultural experts to create linguistic records that preserve both spoken and written forms of endangered languages.
  2. Research and Analysis: Our platform provides advanced research tools for linguists, researchers, and students to analyze and compare linguistic data independently.
  3. Community Engagement and Revitalization: We emphasize community involvement as a core element of our mission, working directly with local communities to facilitate language revitalization.
  4. Advocacy and Awareness: We are committed to raising global awareness about the importance of language preservation and strive to ensure that it becomes a widely recognized part of cultural sustainability and human rights.
  5. Technological Innovation in Language Learning: We integrate innovative technologies like artificial intelligence and speech recognition into our platform to enhance language learning.

A Call to Action

We invite everyone—linguists, educators, enthusiasts, and community members—to join us in this vital mission. Lingua Preserva is an independent platform, and through collaboration and participation, we can ensure that endangered languages are preserved and promoted for future generations.

Together, we will safeguard the rich linguistic heritage of our world. Language preservation is not just about saving words, but about protecting the culture, identity, and history embedded in each language.
