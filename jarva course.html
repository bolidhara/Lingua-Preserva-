Jarawa Language Online Course

Preserve and Learn the Jarawa Language

Module 1: Foundations of the Jarawa Language

Objective: Introduce learners to basic vocabulary, pronunciation, and sentence structures.

Topics Covered:

  • Introduction to the Jarawa Tribe and Language.
  • Phonetics and Pronunciation Basics.
  • Vocabulary: Nature, Family, and Everyday Objects.
  • Simple Sentence Formation and Greetings.

Module 2: Exploring Jarawa Grammar and Syntax

Objective: Enhance understanding of grammar, syntax, and conversational skills.

Topics Covered:

  • Noun Classification, Gender, and Case Markings.
  • Advanced Sentence Patterns (Questions, Negations).
  • Cultural Integration through Greetings and Customs.
  • Vocabulary: Hunting, Weather, and Social Interactions.

Module 3: Advanced Communication and Cultural Narratives

Objective: Develop fluency in advanced conversations and cultural storytelling.

Topics Covered:

  • Complex Sentence Structures and Idiomatic Expressions.
  • Conversational Fluency on Daily Life and Cultural Practices.
  • Exploring Jarawa Myths, Legends, and Songs.
  • Vocabulary: Rituals, Festivals, and Ecological Wisdom.

Module 4: Preservation and Linguistic Research

Objective: Learn techniques for language documentation and preservation.

Topics Covered:

  • Language Documentation Techniques and Tools.
  • Impact of Globalization on Jarawa Language.
  • Teaching the Language: Lesson Plans and Activities.
  • Advocacy for Language Preservation.
